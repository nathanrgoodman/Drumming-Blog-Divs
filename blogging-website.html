New Cymbal // 3-15-20

Today like any other Sunday, I headed to some of the local music shops around my area. While out at one of the shops I came across an early series Zildjian A-Custom series 22" ping ride for only $120! This thing is in great condition and sounds phenominal, in love already after 3 days.

Fixing Snare Wire // 3-03-2020

We have lost a comrad today ladies and gents. It is to my displeasure to inform that my beloved original snare wire has snapped. In between crying and anger, i mustered up the strength to drive to the local guitar center to pick out a new wire. I scanned the wall but alas nothing could come close to the original, all the memories, new ones ceasing to exist...Then I got over myself and bought the cheapest one they had, put it on and it sounds exactly the same.

Here's a little bit about me

Shortly after lessons started, the shop closed leaving me to find other hobbies. I didn't pick up a pair of dumsticks until I was 14, and I immediately knew that was my new found passion.
